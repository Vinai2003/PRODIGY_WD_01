Coffees

Espresso

A strong and concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It's the base for many other coffee drinks.

Latte

Made with one shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam. It's creamy and smooth

Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It's richer than a latte and often topped with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon.

An Americano is a simple yet refined coffee drink, made by diluting a shot (or shots) of espresso with hot water. This results in a coffee that has the depth and complexity of espresso but with a lighter body and a smoother finish, similar to drip coffee.
